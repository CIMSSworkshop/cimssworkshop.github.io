Securing the Future: Advanced Safety and Resilience in Autonomous and Autonomic Systems

Shreekant Thakkar

Abstract: Dr. Shreekant Thakkar's presentation emphasizes the critical role of autonomous and autonomic systems in managing complex, scalable operations like smart cities, while highlighting the increasing security vulnerabilities. He proposes a comprehensive approach to ensure end-to-end security, resilience, and safety in these systems, leveraging Zero Trust architecture and Secure Mesh Shield. The presentation also outlines the operation of Zero Trust Autonomous System Platforms and introduces a framework for autonomic system platform safety, focusing on secure networking, scalability, collective intelligence, and ethical alignment.
